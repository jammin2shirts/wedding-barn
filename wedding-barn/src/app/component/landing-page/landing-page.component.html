The Wedding Barn operates a beatiful event space right in the heart of Central Florida. Located just min 20 north of Orlando, our idyllic setting is a family owned and operated venue that prides itself on creating the perfect event for you. The offer of a rustic setting, stellar customer service, and an inclusive setting are our promise to you. Originally built in 2022, the Wedding Barn features a fully air conditioned barn, cocktail patio, bar area, and a catering prep kitchen. You and your guests will enjoy mingling, and wandering the memorable grounds. As a premier reception venue, The Wedding Barn offers memories to last a life time.

The Ceremony

The Wedding Barn offers a romantic setting for all couples' special occasions. The charming barn can host up to 200 guests under glistening lights and elegant chandeliers. Ceremonies can be held outdoors under the shade of a historic oak tree as you walk down the aisle amoung your closest friends and family. Two pituresque dressing rooms offer comfort and privacy for the whole wedding party as you prepare for your magical day.
